Booking Policies

Payment

A deposit of $20 is required to secure your appointment. Use the link provided to pay. Remaining balance can be paid via Apple Pay, Venmo, Stripe, Zelle, or Cash. NO CASHAPP.

Late Arrival

Please arrive on time. After 20 minutes, appointment will be CANCELED and deposit forfeited. Communicate if you're running behind.

Guests

Clients may bring one respectful guest with 24-hour notice. No kids allowed. Disruptive guests may be asked to leave.

Cancellation

Please cancel 48 hours in advance. No-show/no-call may result in booking restrictions. No cancellation fee otherwise.
